Frequently Asked Questions

Is this meant for real-world usage?

This platform is designed for learning and security awareness. While it follows best practices, production systems require additional layers of monitoring and auditing.

Why is MD5 included if it is insecure?

MD5 is included strictly for educational comparison to demonstrate why fast hash functions are unsuitable for password storage.

Why do strong passwords show extremely large crack times?

Crack-time estimates assume offline attacks with modern hardware. High-entropy passwords can realistically take millions of years to brute-force.
